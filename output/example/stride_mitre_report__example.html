üí° Recommendations and Mitigations

  • Implement strong authentication mechanisms (MFA, strong passwords) for all critical systems and user accounts.
  • Regularly patch and update all software, operating systems, and firmware to protect against known vulnerabilities.
  • Perform regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and remediate weaknesses.
  • Employ intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDPS) and security information and event management (SIEM) solutions for continuous monitoring.
  • Implement data encryption at rest and in transit for sensitive information.
  • Establish robust logging and monitoring to detect anomalous activities indicative of repudiation attempts.
  • Develop and test a comprehensive incident response plan, including data backup and recovery procedures for Denial of Service attacks.
  • Apply the principle of least privilege, ensuring users and processes only have the minimum necessary permissions.
  • Utilize web application firewalls (WAF) and API gateways to protect against common web-based attacks.
  • Segment networks to limit the blast radius of potential breaches.
  • Conduct regular security awareness training for all personnel to counter social engineering and phishing.
